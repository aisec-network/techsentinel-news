Tech Sentinel is a daily cybersecurity news digest with a working engineer's filter — breach disclosures, CVEs that will actually get exploited, ransomware group activity, threat actor intelligence, and the regulatory actions that affect security teams.

## What we cover daily

- **Breach disclosures** — confirmed incidents as they are reported, with affected scope and source links
- **CVE coverage** — vulnerabilities filtered for exploitability, exposure, and available patches
- **Ransomware and threat actor tracking** — active campaigns, group activity, and TTPs worth tracking
- **Regulatory and compliance news** — enforcement actions, fines, and guidance that affect security programs
- **Security tooling and patches** — notable releases, critical patches, and tool updates worth acting on

## Who reads this

Security professionals who want daily coverage of the threat landscape without the noise — practitioners who need to know what happened, what it means, and what to prioritize.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed or newsletter for the daily digest. Tips on unreported incidents or notable CVEs are welcome — contact the editorial desk with a primary-source link.
