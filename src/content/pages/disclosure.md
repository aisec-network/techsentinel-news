---
title: Affiliate disclosure
description: How we earn from outbound links.
---

Some posts on this site include affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps fund the site's research and writing.

We only link to products and services we believe are useful for the audience. Affiliate relationships do not influence editorial coverage — we cover what's interesting and useful, not what pays the most.

Posts containing affiliate links carry an inline disclosure at the top. If you have any concerns about a specific link, email the editor.
