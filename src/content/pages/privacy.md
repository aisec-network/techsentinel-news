title: Privacy
description: What we collect and what we don't.
---

This site collects minimal data:

- Server access logs (IP, user agent, request path) retained for up to 30 days for abuse and debugging.
- Aggregate analytics (page views, referrer, country) via privacy-respecting analytics. No personal identifiers are stored, no cross-site tracking, no advertising cookies set by us.
- Newsletter subscriptions: only your email address, used solely to send the newsletter. Unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.

We do not sell or share data with third parties.

Embedded affiliate links may set their own cookies once you click through to the destination site. That is governed by the destination site's privacy policy.

Questions or removal requests: email the privacy contact listed on the contact page.
